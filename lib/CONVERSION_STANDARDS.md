- **Standard**: International Temperature Scale
- **Authority**: NIST, BIPM
- **Conversion Method**: Non-linear (not ratio-based)

| Unit | Symbol | Conversion to Celsius | Standard |
|------|--------|----------------------|----------|
| celsius | °C | °C | SI Derived Unit |
| fahrenheit | °F | (°F - 32) × 5/9 | Imperial |
